                                                                                              Cyber News Insider

 This application has been developed for my diploma thesis and it's about cybersecurity. 
 Specifically it shows the user the latest cyber news. Also it has lots of categories to choose
 like malware and vulnerabilities and some extra functions like Refresh, Auto update, Save,
 Send by email, Search. The project has been developed with Python and the GUI with Tkinter.

Github: https://github.com/p-vrachnis/CyberNewsInsider
